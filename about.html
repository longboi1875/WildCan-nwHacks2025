Our Mission

Protecting British Columbia communities through innovative wildfire monitoring and early warning systems.

Who We Are

WildCan is a pioneering initiative developed by a team of passionate first-year students at the University of British Columbia. Our goal is to revolutionize how communities respond to wildfires by providing real-time monitoring and instant alerts.

Our Technology

We leverage NASA's FIRMS satellite system to track wildfires across British Columbia in real-time. Our advanced algorithms analyze fire patterns and calculate potential impact zones, ensuring communities receive timely warnings when fires are detected nearby.

Our Impact

By providing instant notifications and detailed fire information, we help communities make informed decisions during wildfire events. Our system has the potential to save lives and protect property by giving residents crucial time to evacuate when necessary.
